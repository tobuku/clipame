About Clipame

The directory for the social media clipping economy.

Clipame comes from the idea of "clip a me" -- creating a clip of me. A place where creators, editors, and audiences connect through short-form content.

Our Mission

The social media clipping industry is one of the fastest-growing segments of the creator economy. Thousands of clippers, agencies, and tools power the short-form content that drives platforms like TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, and X.

Yet until now, there has been no single, neutral source tracking who the major clippers are, which agencies exist, which tools are used, which campaigns are available, and who is hiring.

Clipame exists to fill that gap. We are building the most comprehensive directory and intelligence layer for the global clipping economy -- connecting creators who need their content distributed with the professionals who make it happen.

"Clipame connects creators, clippers, agencies, and tools in one searchable directory. The trusted source for the social media clipping economy."

What is Social Media Clipping?

Social media clipping is the practice of extracting high-performing moments from long-form content and transforming them into short-form videos optimized for distribution across platforms.

Skills Professional Clippers Need

  • Hook creation -- capturing attention in the first 1-3 seconds
  • Retention editing -- keeping viewers watching to the end
  • Caption styling -- animated, readable text overlays
  • Story structure -- beginning, tension, payoff in under 60 seconds
  • Algorithmic distribution -- understanding what each platform rewards
  • Audience psychology -- matching content to viewer expectations

Industry Business Models

Agency Model

Done-for-you clipping. Client pays monthly retainer. Agency handles everything from clip selection to posting.

Marketplace Model

Thousands of independent clippers. Pay per 1,000 views (CPM). Creators post campaigns, clippers apply.

SaaS Platform

Software plus clipper marketplace. Analytics, campaign management, and AI-assisted editing tools.

Distribution Network

Massive networks posting content simultaneously. Focused on brands, podcasts, politicians, sports, and influencers.
